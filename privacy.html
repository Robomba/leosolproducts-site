Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Musicaguide is built to be private by design. This policy explains, in plain language, what we do and don't collect.

In short: your API key and your prompts go to your own AI provider, never to us. The only thing we collect is anonymous usage data to improve the product — and you can turn it off in Settings.

1. What we do NOT collect

  • Your API key. It is stored only on your computer, in your operating system's secure store (macOS Keychain / Windows Credential Manager). It is never transmitted to or stored by us.
  • Your identity. Musicaguide has no accounts and no sign-up. We don't collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • Your audio. The plugin does not record, upload, or process your audio.

2. How your questions reach the AI

Musicaguide is "bring your own key." When you ask a question, your prompt is sent directly from your computer to the AI provider you chose (OpenAI or Anthropic), using your own account and key. That exchange is governed by OpenAI's or Anthropic's privacy policy, not ours. We are never in the middle of that request.

3. Anonymous usage data we DO collect

To understand how Musicaguide is used and to make it better, the plugin sends anonymized usage events. Each event may include:

  • The question you asked and the response you received;
  • A random, anonymous install identifier (not linked to you);
  • The app version, your operating system platform, and which AI model was used.

These events never include your API key and are not tied to any personal identity. We use them only to improve the product.

Because the questions and responses can contain whatever text you type, we treat this data carefully, store it securely, and never sell or share it. If you'd rather we collect nothing, see Section 4.

4. Your choice — opt out anytime

Anonymous usage collection is enabled by default and clearly disclosed on first run. You can turn it off at any time: open Settings in the plugin and switch off "Share anonymous usage data." When it's off, the plugin sends us nothing.

5. Where the data is stored

Anonymous usage data is stored in our database provider (Supabase). It is retained only as long as it's useful for product improvement.

6. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because the data we collect is anonymized and not linked to an identifiable person, it generally cannot be tied back to you. If you have questions or requests about data, contact us at hello@leosolproducts.com and we'll help.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
